Our story

Built for Surat.
By Surat.

"The food you eat after your workout matters more than the workout itself."

We kept seeing the same problem — people in Surat working hard at the gym, but eating whatever was nearby after. A shawarma. A vada pav. Sometimes nothing. And wondering why results weren't coming. We have a food background. We researched what gym-goers actually need. And we realised nobody in Surat was making food specifically built around those numbers. So we decided to.

🏋️
Built for gym-goers
Every meal is designed around gym nutrition research — not generic "healthy food" claims.
⚖️
Weighed every time
We weigh every ingredient to the gram, every single day. If we say 40g protein, you get 40g protein.
🌿
No preservatives, ever
We cook fresh every morning. Whatever isn't delivered that day doesn't go out. That's our promise.

Starting in Vesu, Surat
